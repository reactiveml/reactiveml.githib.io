Now we can run our alarm-clock, 

#run alarm ck_off ck_bip ck_snooze ck_arm;;

and control it via signals ck_arm, ck_snooze and ck_off

First lets arm the alarm with value 500 and wait until the alarm rings. 

emit ck_arm 500;;

Then, one can snooze the clock. It should ring again in one or two seconds. 

emit ck_snooze;;

Finally, one can turn off the clock. 

emit ck_off;;
