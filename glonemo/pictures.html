Example of a Glonemo simulation

The example

The goal of the network is to detect a radioactive cloud. The cloud, modeled in Lucky is a disk.
The routing layer is directed diffusion. (See for more information)
The MAC layer is a preamble sampling mac protocol.

Screenshots of Glonemo simulations

The color of the nodes indicates in which mode (sleep, idle, ...) is their radio. The cloud is the red disk.
